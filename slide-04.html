The Problem Statement

The Challenge Facing KwaZulu-Natal

High Youth Unemployment

Thousands of qualified graduates remain unemployed in KwaZulu-Natal despite possessing valuable skills and qualifications

62.5% Youth Unemployment Rate (SA, 2024)

Skills Gap

Disconnect between academic qualifications and actual industry demands creates a mismatch in the job market

Limited Opportunities

Few pathways for graduates to gain practical, paid work experience that leads to permanent employment

Untapped Potential

A generation of talented, educated graduates represents untapped human capital that could drive regional growth

The Result: Both employers and job seekers remain frustrated while the regional economy suffers from unused talent and potential.
