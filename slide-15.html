Implementation Roadmap

Phased Approach to Launch and Scale

A strategic, milestone-driven implementation plan ensuring successful deployment and sustainable growth

Phase 1

Foundation

Months 1-3 | Q1 2026

Platform Development

  • Complete core platform architecture
  • Develop user registration & authentication
  • Build profile management system
  • Implement project matching algorithm
  • Integrate payment processing

Partnership Establishment

  • Sign MoUs with government departments
  • Onboard 10-15 corporate partners
  • Partner with 3-5 training providers
  • Secure initial funding commitments

Content Development

  • Create initial training courses (AI, Power BI)
  • Develop user guides and documentation
  • Prepare marketing materials
Key Deliverable: Functional MVP platform ready for beta testing

Phase 2

Pilot Launch

Months 4-6 | Q2 2026

Beta Testing

  • Recruit 200 pilot graduates
  • Launch 15-20 initial projects
  • Run 3 training programs
  • Conduct usability testing
  • Gather feedback and iterate

Process Refinement

  • Optimize matching algorithm
  • Streamline payment workflows
  • Enhance user interface based on feedback
  • Establish support procedures

Impact Measurement

  • Track pilot metrics and KPIs
  • Document success stories
  • Prepare case studies
  • Analyze performance data
Key Deliverable: Validated platform with proven concept and early success metrics
↓
Phase 3

Full Launch & Scale

Months 7-12 | Q3-Q4 2026

Platform Expansion

  • Public launch across KZN
  • Onboard 2,000+ graduates
  • Deploy 100+ projects
  • Launch mobile applications (iOS/Android)
  • Expand training catalog to 15+ courses

Partnership Growth

  • Add 30+ new corporate partners
  • Expand government collaboration
  • Partner with all KZN universities
  • Secure international donor funding

Marketing & Outreach

  • Multi-channel marketing campaign
  • University roadshows
  • Media partnerships
  • Social media activation
Key Deliverable: Fully operational platform with significant user base and proven impact
